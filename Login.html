Flat Booking System

A flat booking system is an online platform that allows users to book apartments or other living spaces. It typically includes a searchable database of available properties, a payment system, and a variety of other features. The system allows users to view detailed listings of properties, including pictures, amenities, prices, and more. It also allows users to book and pay for their desired accommodation directly from the system.
